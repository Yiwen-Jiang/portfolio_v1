ART 125-Intro to Graphic Design

For this assignment, we chose an exhibition and created a brochure for it. I chose Alphonse Mucha, my favorite illustrator. Considering his gorgeous style and exquisite coloring, I chose a more artistic typeface. The whole brochure has many large pictures and long texts, so I have made a lot of efforts to balance the space.
